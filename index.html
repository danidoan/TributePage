Actress Audrey Hepburn

"Elegance is the only beauty that never fades."

Here's a timeline of Audrey Hepburn's life

  • May 4, 1929 - Born in Ixelles, Belgium to Dutch Baroness Ella van Heemstra and Joseph Victor Anothy Ruston
  • 1934 - Begins ballet at the age of 5 years old
  • 1939 - England declares war on Germany; Sent to Holland for her safety
  • May 10, 1940 - Germany invades Holland and takes over her village
  • Autumn of 1944 - Food is no longer imported into Holland; Becomes too weak to dance due to malnutrition
  • May 4, 1945 - Holland is liberated from Germany's power on her 16th birthday; Moves to Amsterdam to start doing ballet again
  • 1948 - Appears in her first film as an air stewardess for an educational travel film (Dutch in Seven Lessons); Moces to London and dances in a cabaret show, High Button Shoes, after being told she could not be a dancer due to dramatic weight changes during the war
  • November 21, 1951 - Stars in her first broadway show, Gigi
  • 1953 - Lands her first major role in a movie that was initially meant for Elizabeth Taylor, Roman Holiday
  • 1954 - Begins her humanitarian career with UNICEF
  • September 25, 1954 - Marries Mel Ferrer, a B-movie actor at the time
  • 1960 - Receives an iconic role from starring in the film Breakfast at Tiffany's
  • July 17, 1960 - Sean Ferrer, her first son, is born
  • January 18, 1969 - Marries Andrea Dotti, an Italian socialite
  • February 8, 1970 - Luca Dotti, her second son, is born
  • January 20, 1993 - Passes away from appendiceal cancer

"People in Ethiopia, the Sudan, etc., don't know Audrey Hepburn, but they recognize the name UNICEF. When they see UNICEF, their faces light up, because they know that something is happening. In the Sudan, for example, they call a water pump 'UNICEF.' "

-- Audrey Hepburn
